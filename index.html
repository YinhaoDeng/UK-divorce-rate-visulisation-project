UK divorce rates visulisation


The webpage displays the visulisation of information relative to divorce in England and Wales, from 1971 to 2012 (the latest year when the data was collected).


Author: Yinhao Deng

Date: September 2021

*Remind: It takes time to load charts from the Tableau server. If the chart greyed out, try to click somewhere out of the dashboard area.

Findings:

  • median duration of marriage various between 8.9 (occured in 1985) years to 12.2 years (occured in 1972).
  • The median age at divorce is in a rising trend for both genders.
  • Divorce rate first time increases after 6-year decrease since 2004.
  • There are peaks in the early of each decade, according to reference, they were impacted by recession.

Future work:

  • To explore why there is a rapid growth in divorce rate in 1985. (based on the 'Median age at divorce against gender' Chart).
